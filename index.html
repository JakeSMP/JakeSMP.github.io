About JakeSMP

The JakeSMP is a multiplayer Minecraft server created by Stetup and designed for creativity, collaboration, and community. Whether you're building epic structures, exploring the world, or engaging in friendly competition, JakeSMP is the place to be!

Features

  • Custom plugins for a unique experience
  • Exciting events and competitions
  • Friendly community and active moderators
  • Dedicated survival and creative worlds

Community

The JakeSMP community is welcoming and inclusive. Whether you're new to Minecraft or a seasoned player, you'll find friends and fun here. Join us by applying to be a part of the server!
